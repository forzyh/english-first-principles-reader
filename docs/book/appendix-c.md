# 附录C　综合练习

建议先在纸上完成，再对照附录D。开放题没有唯一措辞，但答案必须保持结构与信息功能。

## C.1 字符串、短语与主干

**1.** 为句子 The careful technician quickly restarted the damaged server. 画一棵简化句法树，至少标出 Subject NP、Predicate VP、Object NP 和三个修饰语。

**2.** 用方括号标出主要短语，并圈出中心词：

> Several highly experienced engineers from our Singapore office have joined the project.

**3.** 下面句子有哪两种主要挂接解释？分别改写消歧。

> She photographed the bird on the balcony.

**4.** 找出每句的句法主语与语义角色：

- The storm destroyed the bridge.
- The bridge was destroyed by the storm.
- There were several cracks in the bridge.
- It was raining heavily.

**5.** 判断粗体成分是补语还是修饰语，并说明理由：

- She put the keys **in the drawer**.
- She found the keys **in the drawer**.
- He became **angry**.
- He spoke **angrily**.

**6.** 从最小主干开始，将下列信息逐层加入一个英语句子：

- 一位年轻研究员发现了一个错误；
- 发现发生在昨晚；
- 错误位于训练数据中；
- 错误导致模型产生偏差；
- 研究员来自数据质量团队。

**7.** 解释为什么 It surprised me that she resigned. 通常比 That she resigned surprised me. 更符合末端重量。两句是否都合语法？

**8.** 判断下列片段在什么语境中自然，并补写一个完整限定句版本：

- Looks promising.
- No need to wait.
- Coming with us?
- Already finished.

## C.2 动词槽位、冠词与引用

**9.** 给下列句子标注基础句型：S+V、S+V+C、S+V+O、S+V+O+O 或 S+V+O+C。

- The child laughed.
- The soup tastes salty.
- She sent me a link.
- They elected Maya chair.
- The update fixed the problem.

**10.** 修正动词槽位错误：

- She explained me the process.
- We discussed about the delay.
- He suggested us to leave early.
- They prevented the visitors to enter.
- I entered into the building.

**11.** 为 open 写出不及物、及物和形容词/结果状态三种构式，各造一句。

**12.** 在空格中填 a/an、the 或零冠词，并解释引用状态：

> I saw ___ unusual device on the desk. ___ device had no visible buttons, but ___ screen on its side was glowing. I asked ___ engineer nearby what it was. She said it was used to measure ___ air quality.

**13.** 解释冠词差异：

- Children need sleep. / The children need sleep.
- We went to hospital. / We went to the hospital.
- She became president. / She met the president.
- Coffee is expensive. / The coffee I bought was expensive.

**14.** 下列代词各可能指谁？改写使指代唯一。

> When Maya called Lena after she received the results, she sounded relieved.

**15.** 找出不自然的引用并修正：

> A customer entered the shop. A customer asked for a refund. The assistant checked a receipt, but it was missing a date. Then a customer called the manager.

假设后续所有 customer 都是同一人，receipt 是该顾客递交的那张。

**16.** 为以下场景各写一句自然英语：

- 第一次告诉朋友，你买了一辆自行车。
- 第二句继续说这辆自行车很轻。
- 你们面前只有一扇门，请对方关门。
- 泛指自行车是一种高效交通工具。

## C.3 介词、修饰与从句

**17.** 选择 in/on/at 并说明概念化方式：

- ___ the station（把车站当行程坐标）
- ___ the station（强调在建筑内部）
- ___ Monday morning
- ___ 8:30
- ___ risk
- ___ trouble

**18.** 选择最合适的路径介词：to, into, toward, through, across, out of。

- The dog ran ___ the gate and disappeared.
- She walked ___ the room and sat down.
- We drove ___ the tunnel.
- He swam ___ the river.
- The child moved ___ the teacher but stopped halfway.
- Smoke poured ___ the window.

**19.** 判断介词短语挂接在名词、事件还是整个命题上：

- The report on the desk is mine.
- She read the report on the train.
- In my view, the report is incomplete.
- The decision to delay the launch was announced on Friday.

**20.** 解释 only 的作用域差异：

- Only the manager approved the plan.
- The manager only approved the plan.
- The manager approved only the plan.
- The manager approved the plan only yesterday.

**21.** 修正悬垂修饰：

- After reviewing the data, several inconsistencies were found.
- Walking through the lab, the alarm suddenly sounded.
- To reduce costs, the office lights were replaced.

**22.** 判断粗体从句属于名词性、定语或状语从句，并标出内部空位或外部槽位：

- **What the team discovered** changed the plan.
- The error **that the team discovered** was serious.
- We changed the plan **because the team discovered an error**.
- I wonder **whether the team discovered the error**.

**23.** 把两个句子合并为含关系从句的一句，并根据含义决定是否加逗号：

- The engineers completed the audit. They work in our Berlin office.（只有柏林办公室的工程师完成）
- Dr. Chen completed the audit. She leads our Berlin office.（补充说明）
- I reviewed a report. You sent the report yesterday.

**24.** 修正嵌入疑问语序：

- Do you know where is the file?
- I wonder why did the system fail.
- Could you tell me when will the meeting start?

## C.4 时态与体

**25.** 选择一般过去或现在完成，并说明参照时间：

- I ___ (finish) the report yesterday.
- I ___ (finish) the report, so we can send it now.
- We ___ (receive) six requests this week.（本周尚未结束）
- We ___ (receive) six requests last week.
- She ___ (live) here since 2020.

**26.** 比较意义：

- I lived in Madrid for five years.
- I have lived in Madrid for five years.
- I had lived in Madrid for five years before moving to Lisbon.

**27.** 选择简单体或进行体：

- Water ___ (boil) at 100°C at standard pressure.
- The water ___ (boil); turn off the stove.
- I ___ (read) a book about syntax this week.
- I usually ___ (read) before bed.
- You ___ always ___ (interrupt) me!（带抱怨）

**28.** 选择现在完成或现在完成进行，并说明焦点：

- She ___ (write) three chapters.
- She ___ (write) since breakfast.
- It ___ (rain); the ground is wet.
- We ___ (solve) the main problem.

**29.** 用过去完成在必要处重写，使时间顺序清楚：

> We arrived at the station. The train left. We called a taxi.

给出两种语境：一是列车在我们到达前离开；二是我们到达后列车才离开。

**30.** 为未来安排选择合适形式，并说明理由：

- 电话响了，你即时决定去接。
- 你已约好周四与客户会面。
- 根据乌云预测要下雨。
- 火车时刻表显示明早六点出发。

**31.** 判断状态动词的构形为何不同：

- I think you are right.
- I am thinking about the proposal.
- She is kind.
- She is being kind today.

**32.** 把下列事件分别写成背景过程与前景事件：

- 下雨；
- 人们赶回家；
- 一辆车突然停在我旁边。

## C.5 情态、条件、否定、疑问与语态

**33.** 判断情态类型：认识、义务或能力/动力。

- She must be in her office; the light is on.
- Employees must wear badges.
- Leo can solve complex puzzles.
- The results may change after review.
- The door will sometimes stick in winter.

**34.** 解释差异：

- You must not leave.
- You do not have to leave.
- You need not have left.
- You should not have left.

**35.** 把命题 she forgot the appointment 分别表达为：低把握可能、强推断、过去批评、过去不必要但已发生。

**36.** 完成条件句：

- If it ___ (rain) tomorrow, we ___ (stay) home.（开放）
- If I ___ (know) the answer, I ___ (tell) you.（当前反事实）
- If they ___ (test) the backup, they ___ (discover) the fault.（过去反事实）
- If I ___ (take) that job, I ___ (live) abroad now.（混合）

**37.** 将直接请求按礼貌距离逐步改写三次：

> Send me the revised file today.

**38.** 把陈述 The team completed the test. 改成：

- 否定句；
- 一般疑问；
- 询问“谁完成”；
- 询问“完成了什么”；
- 强调是“测试”而不是报告。

**39.** 修正助动词错误：

- Does she likes tea?
- Where she did go?
- He doesn’t can swim.

**40.** 把主动句改成更符合给定篇章焦点的被动句：
